06 / 12 Jurisdiction
Structure
The Kilo Au

Gold collateralises. Credit deploys. Discipline returns.

The kilogram is pledged to a senior credit facility at a maximum loan-to-value of seventy-five percent. Drawn capital is deployed by the investment committee into private credit and select pre-IPO positions. Yield, principal, and metal appreciation flow through the waterfall. The collateral does not move.

01 · Collateral Allocated Gold 1 kg LBMA bars per member. Malca-Amit Singapore FTZ. Segregated, serial-numbered. 100% Custody
02 · Facility Senior LTV Line Pledged against the gold pool. Drawn against the LBMA reference price. Floating-rate institutional terms. ≤ 75% LTV
03 · Deployment Private Credit + Equity Investment committee selects sleeve-by-sleeve. Diversified across private credit and pre-IPO positions. 15–25% Gross Tgt
04 · Waterfall Returns Distributed Capital first. Hurdle next. Catch-up. Carry above. European-style waterfall. 8% Hurdle · 20% Carry

Collateral remains in custody throughout · The bar does not move

Target deployment ranges shown are illustrative, gross of timing, and dependent on facility cost, deployment performance, and gold-price path. They are not a forecast or guarantee. Past performance, where cited, does not indicate future results.
