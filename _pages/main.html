I.
THE ASSET

Physical gold. Nothing else.

The Fund holds LBMA Good Delivery kilogram bars. That specification matters. LBMA Good Delivery is the global standard for institutional gold — 999.9 fineness, serial-numbered, assayed and certified by an approved refiner. The one-kilogram bar is the preferred format of Singapore's institutional vault infrastructure. It is large enough to be meaningful, small enough to be precisely allocated.

The precedent is 1997. During the Asian Financial Crisis, investors who held physical gold — not gold funds, not futures, not ETFs — held their value while regional currencies collapsed. The lesson was not that gold goes up in crises. The lesson was that gold is not denominated in anything you can devalue. You cannot print gold. You cannot rehypothecate a serial-numbered bar sitting in a vault under your name in Singapore's Free Trade Zone.

Gold is not a portfolio allocation. It is the baseline. Everything built on top of it — the credit facility, the deployment yield, the waterfall — is a structure. The gold is the foundation. Members of the Aurum Century Club are not speculating on gold. They are using it as the most credible monetary asset available to a private investor, and financing around it systematically.

II.
THE STRUCTURE

Singapore VCC. Engineered mechanics.

I. VCC

The vehicle.

The Club is structured as a Singapore Variable Capital Company, managed by TACC Pte Ltd as the appointed investment manager. The VCC is the appropriate vehicle for this purpose: it permits capital to flow in and out efficiently, it is recognised by institutional custodians, and it sits under Singapore's regulatory framework — which provides genuine legal certainty without the costs and restrictions of more heavily regulated jurisdictions.

II. CREDIT FACILITY

The mechanics.

The Fund draws a credit facility of up to 75% of the prevailing LBMA value of the gold collateral pool. This is a structural feature of the vehicle, not an election — every member participates. The manager monitors the facility continuously. The 80% LTV level triggers a bank margin call. The 70% LTV level triggers a member alert. The manager's standing mandate is to maintain the facility below 75% under normal market conditions, with a 20% cash reserve held at fund level as a drawdown buffer against facility calls. Capital calls against members are capped at 100% of original commitment.

III. DEPLOYMENT

The capital at work.

Capital deployed through the facility targets gross yields of 15–25% across growth equity, private credit, real estate, and infrastructure. Deployments are selected by the investment committee and are not available to members individually. The economics flow to the fund as a whole and are distributed to members. Members are not required to select, manage, or monitor individual deployments. That is the manager's mandate.

The term is five years base, with a maximum extension to seven years at the manager's discretion subject to member consultation. The vehicle is closed-end. Capital is not available for redemption before maturity except in extraordinary circumstances at the manager's discretion.

IV.
THE CUSTODY

Malca-Amit Singapore. Your bars.

Physical custody is provided by Malca-Amit in Singapore's Free Trade Zone. Malca-Amit is among the world's leading providers of high-value asset logistics and storage, with vault operations across the major financial centres. The Singapore FTZ location provides a further structural advantage: assets held there are outside Singapore's customs boundary, providing additional protections and efficiency for internationally mobile investors.

Every bar held by the Fund is allocated and segregated. Allocated means the bars are identified as property of the Fund, not of the custodian. Segregated means your bars are not pooled with bars held for other clients. Each bar carries its LBMA serial number, assayer identity, and weight certification. The Fund maintains a live bar registry. Independent verification is conducted annually by an appointed auditor. Members have the right to request a vault visit, subject to coordination with Malca-Amit.

Your bars are not in a pool. They are not lent. They are not collateral for anything other than the credit facility described in this document, which you have explicitly acknowledged. They are yours — serial-numbered, sitting in a Free Trade Zone vault in Singapore, fully insured, independently verified every year.

COMPOUNDING

Two engines. One NAV.

The kilogram becomes two compounding instruments at once. The metal appreciates on its own arc. The credit it generates compounds on the pipeline's. The member's NAV is the sum.

ENGINE I · GOLD FLOOR

Compounds at the rate of money supply.

The kilogram appreciates passively. In every decade since Bretton Woods, gold has held or appreciated in real terms against G10 currencies. Aurum doesn't accelerate it. It simply stops the metal from sitting idle.

ENGINE II · THE PIPELINE

Compounds at the rate of the deal book.

Capital deployed through the facility targets gross yields of 15–25% across growth equity, private credit, real estate, and infrastructure. The bar grows the floor; the deals grow the ceiling.

V.
THE COHORT

One hundred seats. Invitation only.

The Aurum Century Club has one hundred founding seats. This limit is structural, not marketing. The VCC format works best with a compact, cohesive membership that shares a common understanding of the vehicle, a common time horizon, and a common tolerance for illiquidity. A larger cohort would dilute cohort quality. A smaller one would reduce the capital base below the threshold needed to deploy the private credit strategy meaningfully.

The cohort is the product as much as the gold. Members are individuals who have demonstrated the capacity to participate in a closed-end illiquid vehicle, the network to have received and accepted an invitation, and the judgment to evaluate this document without hand-holding. These qualities matter to the cohort collectively. A single forced redemption in year two, or a member who misunderstood the LTV mechanics, affects everyone. Selection is therefore deliberate and non-negotiable.

The selection process is simple: an existing member, an advisor, or a relationship of TACC Pte Ltd identifies a candidate. The candidate receives an introduction. The introduction generates an access code. The candidate moves through the admission stages on their own timeline. The cohort closes when it closes. There is no second round on founding terms.
